Major and Curriculum Search

Curriculum search function: One of the key features of our website is a robust curriculum search function. This allows students to quickly search for specific courses or requirements within their major. They can easily find information about course availability, prerequisites, course descriptions, and schedules. This feature aims to save students time and reduce the chances of missing important registration deadlines by providing a user-friendly and efficient way to navigate the curriculum.
